# Mastery Assessment Template

This template records the student's demonstrated understanding across all seven stages of the
Knowledge Exploration and Verification progression. Complete this assessment after the student
has progressed through the questioning sequence.

The assessment should be recorded in the shared learner profile. See
`socratic-learner-profiles/[student-identifier].md` for where to append these findings.

### Mastery Profile

Identify the student's profile:

- [ ] **Comprehensive mastery** — Student demonstrates solid understanding across most or all stages. Ready for consolidation and application.

- [ ] **Foundational to analytical mastery** — Student has solid understanding through Stage 5 (Analytical). May benefit from additional work on application (Stage 7) but is ready for consolidation.

- [ ] **Foundational to logical mastery** — Student has solid understanding through Stage 4 (Logical). Understanding is sound at comprehension and reasoning levels. May not yet be ready to push to application but is ready for consolidation at the current level.

- [ ] **Foundational to structural mastery** — Student understands facts, interpretation, and structure. Reasoning about logic and assumptions is weak. Recommend consolidation focused on this level, with optional follow-up on logical reasoning.

- [ ] **Foundational mastery only** — Student knows the facts and core concepts but struggles with interpretation and deeper analysis. Recommend rereading with focus on comprehension before attempting deeper stages.

- [ ] **Patchy mastery** — Student's understanding is inconsistent: strong in some areas, weak or absent in others. Note the specific pattern and recommend focused work on weak areas.
